Why GLP-1?

Most weight loss methods focus only on diet and exercise.

But if your hormones, metabolism, and appetite signals are not balanced – losing weight becomes frustrating and temporary.

That’s where GLP-1 therapy comes in, to address the root cause, which works with your body’s natural hormones to:

  • Control appetite and reduce cravings
  • Promote a natural feeling of fullness
  • Improve blood sugar balance
  • Support sustainable fat loss

Unlike diets that rely on willpower, GLP-1 works on your biology, making weight loss easier and more effective.

How GLP-1 Works?

The Science Behind

GLP-1 (Glucagon-like peptide-1) is a medication that mimics the action of natural GLP-1 in your body to:

Improve insulin response
Better glucose control
Reduce glucagon
Lowers excess blood sugar
Slows down digestion
Stay fuller for longer
Sends satiety signals to the brain
Reduce cravings naturally

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know
1. What is GLP-1 and how does it help with weight loss?
GLP-1 (Glucagon-Like Peptide-1) is a natural hormone your body produces that regulates appetite and blood sugar. GLP-1 medications mimic this hormone to reduce hunger, slow digestion, and help you feel full longer. This leads to reduced calorie intake and sustainable weight loss of 15-20% of body weight on average.
2. Are GLP-1 injections safe for weight loss in India?
Yes, GLP-1-based treatments are clinically tested and widely used under medical supervision. At VIBES, the therapy is doctor-led, carefully prescribed, and monitored, ensuring safety and effectiveness for every individual.
3. Are there side effects of GLP-1?
GLP-1 therapy is generally safe when taken under expert supervision. Some people may experience mild side effects like nausea or digestive changes initially, which usually improve over time. At VIBES, doctors closely monitor your progress to ensure a safe and comfortable experience.
4. How long will I be on GLP-1?
Most patients follow a structured plan from a few months up to a year, depending on goals and progress. Our expert doctors will adjust doses and guide your transition to maintain safe, steady results.
5. How soon will I see results?
Results can vary from person to person based on body type, metabolism, and adherence to the plan. Most individuals begin to notice reduced appetite and gradual weight loss within a few weeks, with visible results over time.
6. Will I be monitored for side effects?
Absolutely. At VIBES, your journey is fully doctor-supervised, with regular monitoring, follow-ups, and adjustments to ensure both safety and optimal results.
7. Do I need to follow a strict diet or exercise routine?
No extreme diets or intense routines are required. You’ll receive a personalised, practical diet and lifestyle plan that supports your treatment-making weight loss more realistic and sustainable.
