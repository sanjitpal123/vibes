Privacy Policy

1. Introduction & Overview

Welcome to Vibes Kolkata (referred to as "VIBES", "we", "us", or "our"). We operate as India's most trusted and advanced wellness and beauty chain. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal and sensitive information when you visit our website, visit our clinics, or use our services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 (SPDI Rules) under the Information Technology Act, 2000 of India.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible wellness and cosmetic services, we may collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Identity Information: Full name, age, date of birth, gender, and photographic images (including before-and-after treatment photos, subject to your explicit consent).
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers, email addresses, residential address, and state/location.
  • Health & Medical Data (Sensitive Personal Data): Medical history, current medications, dietary habits, weight, BMI, skin type, hair condition, allergies, and specific wellness goals (e.g., slimming, skin care, hair regrowth).
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and website interaction data collected via cookies and similar tracking technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following primary purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To customize and provide medical weight management, skin care, laser, and hair regrowth treatments tailored to your specific health profile.
  • Communication: To schedule appointments, send consultation reminders, and respond to your inquiries.
  • Marketing & Promotional Updates: To send you exclusive offers, discounts, and promotional content via SMS, WhatsApp, email, or RCS, provided you have opted in to receive such communications.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze feedback, monitor website usage, and enhance the overall client experience at our clinics.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and medical record-keeping requirements.

4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

Your privacy is our priority. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may only share your information under the following circumstances:

  • With Medical Professionals: Internally among our doctors, dietitians, and specialists to evaluate and administer your treatments.
  • With Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, sending WhatsApp/SMS notifications, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority to disclose information to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.
  • Testimonials: We will only post your before-and-after photos or success stories publicly on our website or social media if we have obtained your prior explicit written consent.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how you use our site, remember your preferences, and serve targeted advertisements. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent; however, some portions of our website may not function properly without cookies.

6. Data Security

We implement rigorous technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your personal and medical information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete health or contact information.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications at any time by replying "STOP" to our SMS/WhatsApp messages or clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in our emails.
  • Withdraw your consent for the processing of your sensitive personal data (note that this may prevent us from providing certain medical or wellness services to you).

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, medical, accounting, or reporting requirements.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
